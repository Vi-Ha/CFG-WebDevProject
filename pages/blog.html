Foods that never expire

Did you know ? Some foods never expire. If the expiration dates are mandatory on the products that we buy, these are sometimes only indicative. After this date, certain foodstuffs such as honey or rice remain edible. To avoid waste, here are some foods to keep (very) long in your cupboards.

Tip: For optimal storage, be sure to keep these products in a cool, dry place, protected from light. Finally, remember to check the quality of your product by tasting it first, to avoid any inconvenience.
